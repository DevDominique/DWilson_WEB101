About the Event

A reading circle for women ready to deepen their sacred wisdom, reclaim their inner power, and awaken their wild feminine essence. Through handpicked books on spirituality, embodiment, and self-discovery, our circle offers an inclusive space for authentic expression, personal growth, and soulful sisterhood.

What to Expect

At Wild Moon, we dont just read books, we awaken their powerful essence. With every title carefully selected in alignment with a celestially-inpired monthly theme, we thrive and flow with the cycles of nature.

  • Inspiration: Read an empowering new book each month
  • Growth: Dive deeper through groups discussions on each title
  • Connection: An intimate group setting to inspire sisterhood.
