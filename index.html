About the Event

We are a North Dallas reading circle for women ready to deepen their sacred wisdom, reclaim their inner power, and awaken their wild feminine essence. Through handpicked books on spirituality, embodiment, and self-discovery, our in-person circle offers an inclusive space for authentic expression, personal growth, and soulful sisterhood at local gatherings.

What to Expect

At Wild Moon, we dont just read books, we awaken their powerful essence through deep discussions on each text. With every title carefully selected in alignment with a celestially-inpired monthly theme and paired with our home-brewed herbal tea, we thrive and flow with the cycles of nature.
